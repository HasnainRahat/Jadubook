Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the platform offer?

JaduBook offers a diverse range of services, including the ability to read books online, listen to audiobooks, and watch video content related to books.

How do I access books on the platform?

To access books, simply login, and browse our library. Click on the desired book to start enjoying the content.

Is there a limit to the number of books I can access?

NO!

We offer all the books just for free.

Can I use the platform on different devices?

Yes, JaduBook is designed to be accessible on various devices. Whether you prefer reading on your computer, listening on your smartphone, or watching videos on your tablet, you can enjoy our content anywhere.

Can I download videos for offline viewing?

Yes, JaduBook allows you to download videos for offline viewing. In the video, in the bottom right corner, there is a three dot sign. If you click it, there are several options including downloading the video, playback speed control, picture in picture format.

How can I provide feedback or report issues?

We welcome your feedback! You can contact us from here or visit the feedback section to share your thoughts, report issues, or suggest improvements. Your input is valuable in making our platform better.
